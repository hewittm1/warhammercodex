ABILITIES

BLINDED BY THE LIGHT

Any ranged attacks made against this character requires an additional +1 BS to land a hit.

Subtract 1 from opponent’s hit rolls that target this unit during the Shooting phase for this unit.

FULMINATE

Killing this character will result in a shard explosion that will deal damage to enemy units up to three inches away. Wounds from this ability will spill over.

If this model is reduced to 0 wounds, roll a 1D6 before removing it from the battlefield. On a 6+, this model will shatter and inflict D3 damage to all nearby enemy units within 6”.

TWINNING

When Different troops are within 7 inches of each other, Sv is 1+ for every different unit Different troops are within 7 inches of each other, Sv is 1+ for every different unit.

If this unit is within 7” of an Anhedral, gains a 5+ invulnerable save.

ENHANCED

Has a chance to increase attack attributes (WS, BS).

If you roll a hit roll of 6+ in the Fight phase, this model’s strength characteristic is increased by 1.

ASSIMILATION

If a Crystal Psyker passes the Deny the Witch Test for an otherwise successfully-manifested enemy psyker power, that Crystal Psyker or any friendly Crystal Psyker within the attempted psyker powers casting distance may attempt to cast that enemy’s psyker ability once.

This unit can attempt to deny one psychic power. If this unit successfully denies a psychic ability, this unit may attempt to manifest an additional psychic power.

DEBUFF

Debuffs enemy units by which it lowers their armor.

Each time you roll a wound roll of 6+ in the Shooting Phase, that hit is resolved with an additional AP of -1.

SCHISM

Has a chance to split an attack between another enemy.

During the fight phase, this model may reroll a single wound roll of 1.

AMBUSCADE

During movement phase, you may remove this model from the battlefield and replace it with a place holder.

At the beginning of your Movement phase, if this unit is not within 1” of an enemy model they can go back underground. Remove this unit from the battlefield. At the end of your next Movement phase, you can return this unit to the battlefield anywhere more than 9” away from an enemy unit. If the battle ends while this unit is underground, they are considered to be slain.
