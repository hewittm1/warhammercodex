X'TAL STRATAGEMS

There should be some sort of flavor text that goes here. This should be about the X'tal Stratagems and a short description of what these are exactly.

2cp

SPITFIRE

Akin to adrenaline and fury within a human being, an X’Tal marksman utilizes ionic excitation to optimize rapidfire.

With a great deal of training in patience and combat, the X’Tal infantry can exert focus to optimize their efforts with ballistic weaponry. Use this Stratagem at the end of your Shooting phase. Select a X’Tal Infantry unit from your army - that unit can immediately shoot again.

1cp

PETRIFY

It is told that the X’tal are indiscriminate of who they leech courage from.

Use this Stratagem at the beginning of the Morale phase. Select a Friendly X’Tal Infantry unit. Enemy units with 3” of that have their leadership reduced by 2.

1cp

ILLUSTRIOUS

In their renowned quick wit and wisdom, a Lustrius can recognise when the time arrives to approach and when to disengage.

Use this Stratagem at the start of your Shooting phase. Select a unit of Lustrius from your army. This unit can immediately move 2D6” but cannot shoot or charge this turn. (“GO! RECON!” Astra *Militarum p. 135).

1cp

INVIGORATING FORCE

One should not so easily underestimate the speed of the Resonites, for epinephrine still flows through these corroded warriors.

Use this Stratagem at the start of your Charge phase. Select a friendly Resonite unit, within 2” of a Ferrosere unit. The unit of Resonite can charge even if they advanced during this turn.
