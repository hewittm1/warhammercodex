ON THEIR TERMS

When this Tactical Objective is generated, your opponent must select an Objective Marker. If you control that objective marker at the end of any turn, score 1 victory point. If you control that objective marker at the end of the turn in which this Tactical Objective was generated, score D3 victory points instead.

12

SPILLING FAMILIAR BLOOD

Score 1 victory point if you destroyed at least one enemy Space Marine unit during this turn. If you destroyed between 3 and 5 enemy Space Marine units during this turn, score D3 victory points instead. If you destroyed 6 or more enemy Space Marine units during this turn, score D3+3 victory points instead.

13

TAKE THEIR TOOLS

Score 1 Victory Point if at least one enemy Vehicle was destroyed but did not explode, crash and burn, etc. during this turn.

14

THE COLLECTIVE'S POWER

Score 1 Victory Point if at least three Psychic Powers were successfully manifested by friendly X'Tal units in your Psychic phase.

15

CRYSTAL CONTAMINATION

Score 1 Victory Point if you control more objective markers than your opponent at the end of the turn.

16

WHAT'S YOURS IS OURS

Score D3 victory points if you control an objective marker that was controlled by your opponent at the start of your turn. If you control 3 or more objective markers that were controlled by your opponent at the start of the turn, score D3+3 victory points instead.
