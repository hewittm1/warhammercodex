TELEPATHIC GAZE

Beings more profoundly err when they sense another’s eyes on them, let alone another’s mind.

Use this Stratagem when an enemy Psyker attempts to manifest a psychic power within 24” of any Gem unit. Your opponent can only roll a single dice for the Psychic test.

1CP

DIRGE OF THE AVALANCHE

An X’Tal procession is a spectacle to behold from a distance.

Use this Stratagem when an Ore unit completes its charge move. Roll a dice for each model in the charging unit that is within 1” of an enemy. For each roll of 6, inflict one mortal wound on an enemy unit within 1”.

1CP

CENOTAPH

May these cadavers of combat serve as a monument for the extinction of hope.

Use this Stratagem during the Morale phase. Select a unit of Jet Obsilisk or Adamantia from your army. Your opponent must add 1 to any Morale tests taken for enemy units within 6” of that unit in this phase.

Illustrious

Even the most experienced warriors can recognise the time when it comes to disengage.

Use this Stratagem at the start of your Shooting phase. Select a unit of Lustrius from your army. This unit can immediately move 2D6” but cannot shoot or charge this turn.

1CP

Psycho Echoes

Although the mortal essence of these combatants have long been forgotten, their spiritual energy remains eternal.

Use this Stratagem when taking a Psychic test for a X’Tal Psyker. Roll three dice rather than two and pick the two highest rolls.

1CP

Glass Ribbons

Retaliation is but an option for even when defeat looks inevitable.

Use this Stratagem in your Movement phase. Select a Silexia unit from your army that is within 1” of an enemy unit. That unit can Fall Back, shoot and charge in this turn.

Ocular Obstruction

With a shimmering glare from our sourceful light, thy vision dims with darkness.

Use this Stratagem during your movement phase. Select a X’tal Glass unit. Until the start of your next Movement phase, enemy models can only shoot this unit if it is the closest visible target.

Merciless

Until victory is assured, ye shall remain relentless!

Use this Stratagem when a Perlumite unit is chosen to attack in the Fight phase. Each time you make a hit roll of 6+ for a model in this unit during this phase, it can immediately make an extra attack against the same target using the same weapon. These extra attacks cannot themselves generate any further attacks.

1CP

Test Thy Will

Only through thy failure can ye become successful.

You can use this Stratagem when a friendly X’tal unit is chosen to fight. If you do so, you can re-roll any failed wound rolls for the unit if the target of the attack has the GEM keyword.
