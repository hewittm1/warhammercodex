WARLORD TRAITS

D6 RESULT

1

ROUND BRILLIANT CUT

Warlords relish in having more reflective faces to further exemplify the horror displayed by their adversaries.

Subtract 1 from the Toughness characteristic of enemy units that are within 6" of your Warlord.

2

UNEARTHED BUT NOT UNGROUNDED

It is this warlord's manner to stow any amount of ammunition possible in its kaleidoscpoic entity.

You can re-roll failed wounds when attacking enemy units with your Warlord.

3

ILLUMINETTING

Opponents of the X'Tal gaze in despondency as bullets vanish in blinding flases of light upon impact, the crystalline surface exposing itself unsathed.

Roll a dixe each time this Warlord loses a wound. On a 6, the Warlord shrugs off the damage and does not lose a wound.

4

ILLUSION BY REFRACTION

Never illuminating its true strategy, a Warlord can contort its prismatic form to guise multiple threats in a the illusion of one.

Roll a dice for each enemy that is within 3" of your Warlord at the start of each fight phase. On a 6, that unit suffers D3 mortal wounds.

5

SMOKE & MIRRORS

An X'Tal Warlord proceeds uts trek relentlessly, only formidable cries of prior victims' agonies more piercing than its material shredding against the terrain.

Re-roll failed charge rolls for your Warlord. In addition, if your Warlord charges in the Charge Phase, add 1 to it's Attack characteristics until the end of the Fight Phase.

6

HOPE DIAMOND

Lesser roles in combat know better than to cast their eyes upon an X'Tal Warlord. Such is a sign of disrespct for the dead and dishonor upon themselves.

Your opponent must subtract 1 from hit rolls they make for any of their Infantry and Beast units that target your Warlord.
